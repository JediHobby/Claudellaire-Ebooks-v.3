The Last Lycan King: His Rebel Mate

Platform: 

Language: English

   Only two pairs have to survive. Cailyn, a young shifter girl who’s tired of her poor life, has to win the Game of a lifetime. A post-apocalyptic age where the werewolf population is on the brink of extinction due to a disease that turns people into cannibalism. To save her mother from the threat of the disease and to get out of the poorest part of their region, Cailyn has to win all the levels of the game set by the New Republic of Carreon, a chance where young shifters can enter the wealthiest city of Carreon and be called as “Champions”. Cailyn has two main reasons to win the game: to live a comfortable life with her infected mother and to assassinate the Alpha King that killed her father. But the Game has so many more surprises than she ever expected. One of these is knowing Xylon Hawthorne—the last descendant of the Primordial Lycan Alpha bloodline. Just like her, he seeks revenge against the people who massacred his whole family.
