The Pregnant Luna's Second Chance Mate

Platform:     

Language: English

  Astrid is a good daughter, a caring sister, and a model student at Maxon University. But her world was ruined when a group of male shifters abducted her and made her life miserable. What made it worse was that one of those men was her mate, Lawrence Wright, the wealthiest guy on their campus, who made her life a living hell after a devastating rejection that almost drove her crazy. All her suffering almost ended that one tragic night. Astrid was dying and had lost most of her memory. By the Moon Goddess' mercy, she was saved by a man who took care of her. Her second chance mate, who claims her as his wife. She fell in love with his kindness, only to find out that he was the brother of the man who tried to kill her. She came back for revenge and became one of the hottest students at their university. Her plan was to make Lawrence beg to his knees. But then she met the man who saved her. And his name was Simon. Simon Wright. And she was carrying his child. Will Astrid's love for Simon be enough to forgive him for his sins?
