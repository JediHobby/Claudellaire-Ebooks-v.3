Trancendence

Platform: 

Language: English

  This is Book One of the Trancendence series. Cassiopeia, a 17-year-old girl was experiencing mysterious changes in her teenage life as her strange dreams turned out to be a manifestation of her emerging power as an heir to the Throne of the Queen of Fae folks, Ellyllon, Protector of the Gates of Realm. Cassiopeia, together with her friends unveils the melancholic path to fulfill her obligation as the next Queen to save humans from the invading demons. Before everything was set about into huge chaos, Cassiopeia found out that her boyfriend, Jared, was an Elven Prince who missioned to assassinate her before her ability awakens and discover her true origin. But Jared fell in love with her and chose to protect Cassiopeia against Estel, the Moon mistress from the Kingdom of Elves, another heir to the throne. Estel, in alliance with the Knaves, has turned into a huge competition with them in finding the keystones for a detrimental cause: to release the demons from The Gate escaping and threatening to destroy the Human Realm as revenge to Queen Ellyllon. But while Jared was away sorting out the situation, Dillon a werewolf, one of Cassy’s guards, was drifting towards her, ready to lay his own life just to keep her safe, not because of his duty but because he secretly loved her from the time they first met…
